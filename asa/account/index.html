What About Security

Your privacy is important to us. Rest assured that we do not transmit or have access to your login information. We use the credentials you provide to fetch data from the Apple Search Ads API and they are securely stored in your macOS keychain. Your information is kept strictly confidential and will only be used for the purpose of accessing the API.
